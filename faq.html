FAQ

What is Chromstack? +

Chromstack represents an innovative e-commerce platform tailored for digital creators and vendors, empowering them to showcase and sell their digital products to a broader audience. Our platform integrates an affiliate program, enabling registered members to earn commissions by referring buyers. We prioritize a seamless and secure customer experience while providing intuitive interfaces for vendors, affiliates, and customers alike.

How Can You Earn with Chromstack? +

At Chromstack, you have the opportunity to generate income as an affiliate by promoting products listed within our marketplace. Additionally, you can earn supplementary rewards through participation in our sales contests. For vendors, revenue is generated through affiliate-driven sales of their products. Moreover, vendors can amplify their earnings by hosting contests for their products, inviting affiliates to participate.

Who Can Join Chromstack? +

Joining Chromstack is accessible to everyone, regardless of technical expertise or specialized training. All you need is a mobile device and an internet connection to get started on our platform.

What Sets Chromstack Apart? +

Chromstack distinguishes itself through the strategic implementation of cutting-edge technological tools and meticulously crafted business models designed to foster the success of our valued affiliates. We offer lifetime mentorship programs, workshop sessions, and streamlined payment packages for our affiliates, ensuring their continuous growth and prosperity.

Is Chromstack Legitimate? +

Rest assured, Chromstack operates as a fully registered affiliate company under the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC), providing legal documentation and registration numbers for authenticity verification. We emphasize sustainable wealth-building practices, distancing ourselves from Ponzi schemes or "get rich quick" schemes..

What is your affiliate program about? +

Our affiliate program allows individuals or businesses to earn a commission by promoting and referring customers to our website to purchase digital products.

How does the affiliate program work? +

Once you join our affiliate program, you'll receive a unique affiliate link. You earn a commission on every successful referral.

How do I access the digital courses after purchase? +

After a successful purchase, you will receive an email with instructions on how to access your digital product. Additionally, you can also log in to your account on our website, where you'll find a section to manage your purchased products.

How can I become a vendor on your platform? +

To become a vendor on our platform, you can start by filling out a vendor application form on our website. Our team will review your application, and if approved, you will receive further instructions via email on how to set up your vendor account.

What if I encounter any issue with my purchased digital course? +

We strive to provide a seamless experience, but if you encounter any issues with your purchased digital product, such as technical difficulties or missing files, please contact our customer support at support@chromstack.com. We will assist you in resolving the problem and ensuring you receive the product as intended.

How can I contact your support team for assistance? +

You can reach out to support@chromstack.com for any inquiries.

Can I request for a refund on a purchased digital course? +

Refund policies may vary depending on the nature of the digital product. We have a 30-day policy period to return purchases.

How and when do I get paid as an affiliate? +

We offer various payment options, including bank transfer or other preferred payment methods. Payment schedules and thresholds are clearly outlined in the affiliate program terms.

How much commission can I earn as an affiliate? +

Our affiliate program offers competitive commission rates. The specific commission percentage or amount will be provided upon joining the program.
